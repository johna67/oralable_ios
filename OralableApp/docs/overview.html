Two Apps, One Ecosystem

Oralable empowers individuals, Oralable for Professionals enables collaboration.

The Oralable Ecosystem

Oralable is a two-app system designed for seamless collaboration between individuals monitoring their own wellness and the professionals who support them.

Oralable (Consumer App)

For individuals monitoring their own muscle activity.

  • Connect to Oralable device via Bluetooth
  • Record muscle activity sessions
  • View your personal session history
  • Export and share data as CSV
  • Demo Mode to explore without hardware

Oralable for Professionals

For therapists, researchers, and coaches.

  • Import participant CSV data
  • Manage multiple participants
  • Analyze sessions and view trends
  • Track progress over time
  • Demo Mode with sample participant data
