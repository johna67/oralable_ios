Features

PPG Sensor Monitoring

Advanced photoplethysmography technology captures muscle activity data with precision.

Real-Time Dashboard

View your muscle activity metrics in real-time with an intuitive, easy-to-read interface.

CSV Data Export

Export your recorded sessions as CSV files to analyze or share with healthcare providers.

Session History

Review past monitoring sessions and track your patterns over time.

Bluetooth Connection

Seamlessly connect to the Oralable sensor via Bluetooth Low Energy.

Local Data Storage

Your data stays on your device. You control when and how to share it.
