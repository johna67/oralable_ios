Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

JAC Dental Solutions Limited ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Oralable mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

The Oralable app collects the following types of data:

  • Sensor Data: Muscle activity measurements from the Oralable sensor, including PPG (photoplethysmography) readings
  • Session Data: Timestamps, duration, and metadata for monitoring sessions
  • Device Information: Device model and operating system version for app compatibility

How We Store Your Data

All sensor and session data is stored locally on your device. We do not automatically upload your health data to external servers.

You control your data. You can export it as CSV files or delete it at any time from within the app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal health data with third parties. Your data is only shared when:

  • You explicitly choose to export and share CSV files
  • Required by law or legal process

Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple HealthKit: Optional integration to store wellness data (requires your permission)
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the app

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data stored on your device. Data transmitted via Bluetooth uses standard encryption protocols.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored in the app
  • Export your data at any time
  • Delete your data from the app
  • Withdraw consent for optional features

Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for use by children under 17. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
